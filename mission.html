Our culture is simple...

We live and breathe rockets... n such. Which is why our name is simply inspired, Rockets N Such. If you were the kid that made cardboard boxes into rockets or had glow in the dark stars on your ceiling, then you're in the right place. Even if you didn't have those things, you're probably still in the right place. From space exploring rockets to simple rockets that just exit the atmosphere, we believe all kinds of rockets are simply cool.
